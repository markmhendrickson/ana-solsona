Who

My name is Ana Solsona and I'm a freelance Graphic Designer and Art Director with a passion for the details.

My practice is based upon creating long-standing collaborative partnerships built on honesty and purpose. I work on an international level and my work ranges from digital design and branding to art direction.

In my former career I had the privilege of collaborate with brands and agencies such as Proximity Worldwide - BBDO Group, Chips Ahoy!, Oreo, Affinity-Petcare, Grupo Mass Chile, Integral Innovation, Gas Natural Fenosa, Pippa Lynn and many more.

What

  • Ana Solsona studio develops visual identities, brand strategies, websites, printed design and packaging, art direction, communication and much more.
  • I deliver digital, print and brand experiences. Working alongside a wide range of clients, from global established companies to start-ups, my aim is to create exciting and meaningful experiences, both online and offline.
  • Think of me as more of a creative partner than a resource. This means we have shared perspective on how we can work together to achieve your goals.
